Introduction

Hello

Hello, I am Collin Leedy, a student at PTC who is learning how to make webpages. Video games is one of my hobbies, so I have decided to create a website about the different video games I have played. I do not play very often these days because I am a full-time student, but I still try to find time to play when I can.

I mainly play single-player games, but I will occasionally pick up a multiplayer game to play with my friends if they also have it. Feel free to suggest games for me to play by sending an email to cjl19@students.ptcollege.edu!
