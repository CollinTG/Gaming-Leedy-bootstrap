Gaming Platforms

So Many Options!

The Possibilities are Endless!

There are seemingly endless ways to play a video game. There are consoles, PC, and handheld devices, but there are also some less common ways to play games these days. For example, emulation has been growing in popularity as a way to play older games without having to buy the original device or operating system it was made for.

On PC, the platform I primarily play games on, it gets even more complicated. There are so many different operating systems, hardware choices, anti-cheat restrictions, and possibly new technologies that could make it harder to find games that support all PC gamers.
